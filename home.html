How to Stay Fit

Try to do at least 30 minutes of exercise every day. You can choose activities that you enjoy, such as walking, running, cycling, or swimming so that your body becomes fitter and healthier

Healthy Eating Habits

Avoid instant food, instant food often contains a lot of sugar, salt and preservatives that can damage the body's health. Eat a balanced diet of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean protein, and low-fat or non-fat dairy products.

Mental Health Matters

Don't hesitate to seek support from family, friends, or a mental health professional if you are having problems. Find ways to manage your stress, such as meditation, yoga or calming activities such as reading or writing.
